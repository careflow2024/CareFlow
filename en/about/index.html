About us

Last updated: June 21, 2026

CareFlow is a family medication management app developed by Sion Studio. We help households stay on top of doses, inventory, and care records with clear reminders and thoughtful design.

You can create medication plans for family members (such as babies, elders, pets, or yourself), schedule dose reminders, log intake and stock changes, and optionally sync across devices when signed in.

What we care about

  • Making complex medication routines simple to follow
  • Respecting your health data with local-first storage and optional sync
  • Improving the product based on real user feedback
